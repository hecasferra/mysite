Studio 88

Debut novel · 2024

Studio 88 tells the story of two colleagues drawn into a slow-burning connection inside a creative workspace where ambition and proximity blur every boundary. What begins as subtle tension, a glance, a lingering conversation, grows into something deeper, riskier, and impossible to ignore.

At its core, the novel explores power, secrecy, and the emotional cost of wanting someone you can't fully have. It's about restraint stretched to its limit, and what happens when desire refuses to stay contained.

Studio 88 is for anyone who believes the most dangerous relationships don't explode. They simmer.
